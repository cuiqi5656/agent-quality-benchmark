# Code of Conduct

AQB follows the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

We commit to a harassment-free, welcoming community regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity or expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

Constructive behavior includes showing empathy, respecting differing viewpoints, giving and accepting specific feedback, and prioritizing the community. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, sexualized conduct, insults, threats, stalking, publishing private information, or other conduct inappropriate in a professional setting.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary. Report conduct concerns privately through the repository owner’s GitHub profile. Reports will be reviewed promptly, confidentially, and without retaliation.
